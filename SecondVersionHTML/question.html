How long should I set grouping period to?

Grouping is when you and other buyers of this item form a group. The longer the grouping period is, the more time you have to allow others to join you and form a large groupbuy. The longest period you can set for grouping is 29 days.

How long should I set bidding period to?

Bidding period is when retailers can place bids on your purchase intention. The longer you allow retailers to bid, the more retailers may bid on your intention and the better price you may get resulting from competition. The longest period you can set for bidding is 29 days.
