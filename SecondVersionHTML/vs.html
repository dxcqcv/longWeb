How Selling on Buckete Works

As a qualified vendor, you will be able to choose which method to sell to customers: instant or manual.

Instant:

1. Upload your inventory to Buckete.com and set a price or range of prices.

2. Customers’ view and post intentions for your products and offer the price they would like to pay.

3. Prices that fall within your set range are processed immediately.

4. Buckete will notify you by email. Simply pack and ship your item to the customer.

5. Once the product is delivered, Buckete deposits the payment into your bank account.

Manual:

1. Select product (or product categories) you are able to sell.

2. Customers post intentions and submit the price they would like to pay.

3. You will receive an email if an intentions falls under your product offerings.

4. Follow the link to Buckete.com and submit your most competitive price.

5. If you submit the most appealing offer, your product is sold! Simply pack and ship your item to the customer

6. Once the product is delivered, Buckete deposits the payment into your bank account.

You are able to switch between these two methods in your account settings. Selling on Buckete is easy!
